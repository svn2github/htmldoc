--headingfont typeface

The --headingfont options sets the typeface that is used for headings in the document. The typeface parameter can be one of the following:

typefaceActual Font
ArialHelvetica
CourierCourier
HelveticaHelvetica
MonospaceDejaVu Sans Mono
SansDevaVu Sans
SerifDejaVu Serif
TimesTimes
